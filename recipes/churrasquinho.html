Churrasquinho

The churrasquinho is a street food in brazil that you can find in every corner, literally. It's a mini-barbecue or like a greek barbecue, great at any situation.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Stick
  • Farofa
  • Sauce

Steps:

  1. Ok, so this one is pretty straight-forward, first you need to have a grill
  2. Then, put the meat into the stick
  3. Cook until it's medium-rare
  4. Brush the sauce on the barbecue
  5. Dip the barbecue in the farofa
  6. here we go, you're ready to eat!
