Miojo

This is a prestigious recipe in the brazilian community, a classic between college students that don't have money to eat properly.

Ingredients:

  • Miojo
  • Water

Steps

  1. Grab a pot
  2. FIll the pot with water
  3. Boil the water
  4. Open the miojo package
  5. Put it inside the pot, and mix until they're loose
  6. Cook for about 3 minutes and put in on a plate
  7. Open the seasoning package and mix with the miojo
