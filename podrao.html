Podrão

Podrão is a classic style of the art of burger-making in brazil, it feels like the best food ever when you just came out of a party or when you're hungry past midnight. Basically it consists in throwing whatever you want and how much you can between the slices of bread.

Ingredients:

  • The worst kind of bread you can find
  • Meat that you don't know the origin neither the quality
  • Sometimes sausage
  • Cheese
  • LOTS of shoestring potatoes
  • Banana
  • Some salad just to maintain it healthy
  • Egg
  • Ham

Steps:

  1. Find the dirtiest and greasiest grill
  2. Fry everything except the salad
  3. DON'T SEAL THE BREAD (VERY IMPORTANT, YOU WANT TO EVERYTHING STICKING TO THE BREAD)
  4. Mount the burger
  5. Put lots of green mayo on the burger
  6. All of this seems gross, but trust me in this one, its gold.
